Minecraft Academy is a website with lessons and

tips that will help you become a better pvper.


Our Goal

Our goal is to teach new players how to pvp in Minecraft, and to try to teach more advanced techniques to players who already play the game and are trying to get better. No matter what your skill level, there are many things to learn including things from different game modes, so whether you are learning how to w tap or learning how to bridge faster in bedwars, we may have something that can help you improve in pvp.
